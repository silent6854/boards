💡 Tips & Help

Add notes: right‑click board → "Add Note".

Move board: drag empty space or use scroll/pinch to zoom.

Connect notes: right‑click note → "Connect to Another", then click another note.

Disconnect: right‑click → "Disconnect..." then choose a connected note to remove that single link, or "Remove All Connections".

Customize: left‑click note (not on text) → change color, font, size, direction, layer order.

Star: click the star icon to mark important notes.

Tags: View → "Show Tags" to see auto‑generated #tags above notes.

Search: use the 🔍 button to find notes by title, tag, or content.

Boards: up to 5 boards, rename by double‑clicking tab name.

Export/Import: File menu for saving/loading board data.
